CTE

CTE, or Career Technical Education, is a subset of classes offered at Sprague High School that prepares, teaches, and empowers students in a variety of subject areas through hands on learning. Students who take these classes not only learn the fundamentals to persue their area(s) of intrest, but depending on the class, the student may be able to recieve certifications to have an advantage over non CTE Schools.
